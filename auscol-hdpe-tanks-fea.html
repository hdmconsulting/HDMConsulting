AUSCOL HDPE Tanks FEA

This project involved performing a detailed Finite Element Analysis (FEA) on an HDPE tank subjected to negative pressure. The goal was to examine the impact of incorporating an internal steel frame to enhance the tank's durability and material efficiency.

Key Details:

Location: Melbourne, Victoria, Australia
Duration: February 2020 - Present (4 yrs 5 mos)
Technologies Used: CAD, FEA, CFD, HAZOP study

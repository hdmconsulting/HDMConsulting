About Us

HDM Consulting is an Australian engineering services company that specialises in providing industrial solutions for the process industry. Our mission is to maximise value for our clients through our comprehensive range of services. Formed in 2020, we have quickly established ourselves as a trusted service provider to the manufacturing, energy, mining, and mineral processing industries. We pride ourselves on expanding our client base by forging and maintaining strong relationships with both new and existing clientele. Our expertise in CAD, FEA, CFD, and HAZOP studies ensures that we deliver innovative and efficient engineering solutions tailored to meet the unique needs of each project.

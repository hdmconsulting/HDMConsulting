Finite Element Analysis of a Vacuum Frame for HDPE Tank

This project involved performing a detailed Finite Element Analysis (FEA) on an HDPE tank subjected to negative pressure. The goal was to examine the impact of incorporating an internal steel frame to enhance the tank's durability and material efficiency.

Key Details:
Location: Melbourne, Victoria, Australia
Duration: February 2020 - Present (4 yrs 5 mos)
Technologies Used: CAD, FEA, CFD, HAZOP study

Project Highlights:

  • Design Optimization: Analyzed three primary frame profiles with Profile C emerging as the most efficient.
  • Simulation and Analysis: Utilized Ansys Mechanical for simulations to ensure stability and structural integrity.
  • Findings: Internal frames significantly enhance the tank's ability to withstand negative pressure, with Profile C offering the best balance between performance and material cost.
  • Benefits: Improved structural support, reduced material usage, enhanced safety, and operational efficiency.
